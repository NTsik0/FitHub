About This Gym

Gladiator Fitness is Tbilisi's premier CrossFit and strength training facility. Founded in 2018, the gym has earned a reputation for world-class equipment, elite coaching, and an unmatched training atmosphere. Whether you're an absolute beginner or a seasoned athlete, Gladiator has a program for you.
